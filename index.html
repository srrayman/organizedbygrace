the story

grace is a florida native, currently living in sunny miami. growing up she was always meticulous in her ways. from making sure her bed was made every morning to spending hours lining up her trolls dolls just right, her appreciation for organization runs deep. her mission for starting organized with grace was to bring her love for aesthetics into the homes of her community by introducing a simple and systematic way to get and stay organized.

the strategy

have you been married for 25 years and need to purge and refresh many areas of your house? are you a bachelor who just moved into your first apartment with no roommate? grace is here to help!

while prices are listed, grace is willing to work within your budget, as everything is customizable. if you love the look of plastic bins or want to keep your space super simple, all of grace’s plans are bespoke and geared towards results that her clients’ personally want to achieve.

grace’s customized plans will categorize and merge your style with a practical design while reminding you to never underestimate the power of tidy spaces. regardless of your preferred aesthetic, grace will work with you personally to create a plan that meets your needs both practically and aesthetically. you will sit down (in person or virtually) and discuss your ideas in depth. she will send an outline of the project(s) so you know what is going on every step of the way. grace will measure each and every shelf to make sure all your items will fit perfectly if you choose to purchase extras. of course, any changes throughout the process are completely fine, this is your space, after all! thank you for trusting organized with grace.

grace is very excited to have been invited to your home!
